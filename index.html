The Manual for Mr. Cakez:

1. Traverse to directory where the bot is located. For example "D:\Spieleentwickler\TwitchBot\".
2. Open terminal window in current directory.
  2a. Click on the address bar at the top of the window.
  2b. Delete everything in the address bar.
  2c. Press keys "c", "m", "d" on the keyboard one after another to put "cmd" in the address bar.
  2d. Press enter.
  2e. New window should pop out, it's the terminal window.
3. Inside the terminal put "git pull" text just like you did in step 2c. And press enter.
4. Inside the terminal put "dotnet build" text just like you did in step 2c. And press enter.
5. Close terminal window, it's not longer needed.
6. Traverse to bot compile output directory. For example "D:\Spieleentwickler\TwitchBot\bin\Debug\net7.0-windows\win-x64\".
7. Don't show next steps on the screen.
8. Open "Secrets.ini" file in text editor of your choice. Can even be 4looser.
9. Find a section which starts with "; ----- Twitch".
10. Inside that section perform these steps:
  10a. Replace "Name =" with "TwitchName =".
  10b. Replace "CustomerID =" with "TwitchClientID =".
  10c. Replace "Password =" with "TwitchPassword =".
11. Save changes.
12. Double click left mouse button over "AbevBot.exe" line in the directory you traversed in step 6.
13. Good job you have successfully updated the bot.
